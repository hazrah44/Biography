Banana Pancake

Pancake are one of my favorite foods. Here is a recipe for tasty pancake:

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 2 bananas
  • 2 tsp of vanilla extract
  • butter
  • 2 tbs of milk

Method

  1. Melt butter in a frying pan over a medium heat
  2. Gently mix the ingredients in a bowl
  3. Once butter has melted add the pancake mix
  4. Using a spatula flip from the edge of the pancake to the center every 30 seconds (as if you are making an omelette
  5. When the pancakes are still hot add maple syrup and a little bit of butter
  6. Enjoy!!!!
